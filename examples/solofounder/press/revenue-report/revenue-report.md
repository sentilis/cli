# Month 1: $1,240 MRR

The first month of TaskFlow is officially in the books. I'm shocked by the response from the community.

## The Numbers

- **Unique Visitors:** 5,400
- **Signups:** 450
- **LTD Conversions:** 12
- **Revenue:** $1,188 (LTDs) + $52 (Subscriptions)

## What I Learned

1.  **Distribution is everything.** 80% of traffic came from a single X (Twitter) thread.
2.  **LTDs are great for cash flow.** It allowed me to pay for my hosting for the next 2 years.
3.  **Support is time-consuming.** I need to automate some of the onboarding.
